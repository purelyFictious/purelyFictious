Hello, loves!
Books, as they say, are the soul of imagination. Well, I don't know if anyone actually says that, but go with it.
On this wonderful page, I will post book reviews about books that I have read (it may take a bit to catch up to date), and will rate them so that you don't have to go through the excruciating process of painfully sifting through the bad ones.
Mind you, I'm not a very judgemental person, so there probably won't be too many critiques on my part. But I'll be sure to list the weird things that the book may hold inside its possibly (most likely) weird contents, so that you'll know if its for you.
If you want me to do a certain book, go to the contacts page, and send me an email! If you don't have anything specific in mind, still email me through the contacts page!
Believe it or not, I actually like hearing from you weirdos, so send me an email everybody.
Yours sincerely,
